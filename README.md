
# Global Superstore Sales Analysis

## Project Overview

* This project analyzes global sales performance for a multinational retail company, Global Superstore, operating across multiple markets and continents.
* The objective is to provide senior management with a clear, data-driven view of business performance to support market expansion and product strategy decisions.
* An interactive Power BI dashboard was developed to answer key strategic questions around overall performance, regional markets, and product categories.

## Business Objectives

Senior management aims to:

* Understand the current overall business performance

* Evaluate sales and profitability across global markets

* Identify high- and low-performing product groups to guide strategic focus

## Dataset Structure

The dataset consists of three relational tables:

* Orders: Transaction-level data including sales, profit, quantity, product, and market information

* People: Sales representatives assigned to different regions

* Returns: Records of returned orders

These tables were modeled and connected to enable comprehensive performance analysis.

## Key Business Questions Answered

* What is the current overall business performance?

* Total sales, profit, order volume, and trends over time

* How are different markets performing?

* Sales and profit by region and country

* Identification of growth and underperforming markets

* How are product categories and sub-categories performing?

* Revenue and profitability comparison

* Strategic product groups contributing most to business growth

## Dashboard Features

* KPI cards for Sales, Profit, Orders, and Profit Margin

* Regional performance analysis by market and country

* Product category and sub-category performance breakdown

* Time-based trend analysis

Interactive filters for market, region, and product group
